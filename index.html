Jean Pinsolle

Postdoctoral researcher at SONDRA (CentraleSupélec)

Email: jean.pinsolle@centralesupelec.fr

Research interests

Statistical signal processing, machine learning, inverse problems, anomaly detection, optimization, diffusion models and flow matching.

My current research focuses on the analysis and adaptation of modern generative models for signal and image processing, with interests in Bayesian methods, stochastic modeling, uncertainty quantification and radar applications.

Current position

Since June 2025, I am a postdoctoral researcher at SONDRA (CentraleSupélec / ONERA), funded by the ANR-NEPTUNE3 project.

I also participate in the co-supervision of research projects related to anomaly detection and SAR image generation.
